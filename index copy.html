Sound Forest by The Sea

Sound Forest by The Sea (SFBS) is an interactive, beach-inspired soundscape. In the soundscape, the user can play with waves and scare of visiting birds. Utilizing sound- and distance data, various parameters such as sound pitch, amplitude, as well as string colour are controllable.

Steammind

Inspired by Steam's recommendation system, we approached a new way of visualizing your Steam library. By displaying games that you own, Steammind aims to both provide insight on your personal gaming preferences as well as visualize a selected set of other games that fits your current library.

Spir: Take a breather with a smart coaster

Working with the general theme of Pandemic Media, our goal was to explore different ways to take a break in a working-from-home environment. Looking at the natural pauses of food-breaks, we investigated how to utilize these breaks to connect with your food and be more mindful.
